About the Fatwa Repository

Prof. Abdullahi

Prof. Abdullahi is a renowned Islamic scholar with decades of experience in issuing fatwas and providing guidance on Islamic jurisprudence. His expertise spans various aspects of Sharia, making him a trusted authority in the Muslim community.

Purpose of the Repository

The Fatwa Repository is a digital platform dedicated to preserving and sharing Islamic rulings. Our mission is to provide accessible, authentic, and well-organized fatwas to guide Muslims worldwide in their daily lives.

Project Goals

  • Create a comprehensive archive of fatwas for easy access.
  • Promote understanding of Islamic rulings through clear and reliable sources.
  • Support the global Muslim community with accurate and timely guidance.
